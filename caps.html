Mental Health and Counseling Services

We value wellness and understand the importance of students maintaining their physical and mental health throughout their time at UMSI. There are many campus and community resources available to support your overall well-being.

Let's Talk

“Let's Talk” is a free program that supports students starting their mental health journeys. It provides casual meetings with counselors during specific drop-in times for initial support and advice.

Individual Peer Counseling

Individual Peer Counseling (IPC) provides a fresh approach to addressing the needs of students at the University of Michigan. Our goal is to support student mental health by offering a safe and welcoming space for students to share their thoughts and feelings.

First Year Guide

We are excited to welcome all first-year students, whether you are living on campus or joining us remotely. To support your transition, we created a comprehensive resource designed to help you navigate this exciting new chapter and feel at home in your journey.

Scope of Services

Whether you are a new student or about to graduate CAPS has a variety of services to help ensure that your time at UMSI is sucessful.If you have further inquiries don't hesitate to contact us directly for even more information about our services.
