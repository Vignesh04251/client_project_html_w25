Academic Advising Information

At the University of Michigan School of Information (UMSI), we are committed to supporting our students' academic success by offering a wide array of resources and services. Our academic advising team provides personalized guidance to help you navigate your curriculum, select courses, and address any academic challenges or questions you may encounter during your studies. UMSI students also have access to the extensive collections and study spaces within the University Library system, offering both digital and physical resources essential for research and coursework. Additionally, our specialized tutoring services and the UMSI Writing Center are available to support you in mastering complex subjects and enhancing your written communication skills. By utilizing these academic resources, UMSI students can thrive in their studies and achieve their educational goals.

Academic Advising

Academic Advising at UMSI empowers students to envision, execute, and reflect on their academic journey with the support of a qualified advisor. Through collaborative discussions about goals, challenges, and questions, students gain guidance on university and program requirements, policies, and processes. Together, advisors and students create an academic plan tailored to personal and professional aspirations

Degree Progress and Registration

The MSI degree consists of 39 credit hours which must be earned at the school of information. You are allowed to take electives out side of SI but you must complete the minimum 39 SI credit requirments. SI students on the mastery course track must complete a set of required prerequitie classes in order to be eligible to take the final capstone class.

Tutoring, Waivers, and Support Resources

At UMSI, we are committed to supporting your academic success through a variety of resources, including tutoring, waiver options, and additional support services. Whether you need help mastering course material, navigating academic requirements, or accessing resources to enhance your learning experience, we are here to help. Our tutoring services provide personalized guidance to strengthen your understanding of key concepts, while waiver options offer flexibility to meet specific academic needs. Additionally, our support resources are designed to ensure you have the tools and assistance necessary to achieve your goals and thrive during your time at UMSI. Together, we are here to help you succeed and make the most of your academic journey.
